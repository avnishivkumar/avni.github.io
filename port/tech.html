TECH TALES (2019)

An interactive art piece that is meant to literally shed light on the role of women in tech industries. Although still a work in progress, the user uses their hands as tools to move around the source of light and read the information on the shapes. Each shape has a different fact or stastic about the bias against women in the tech industry. The piece is important to me as I drew inspiration from not only myself, but also my classmates who were all women. The piece is one of my first art pieces using Open Frameworks. Since this piece is a work in progress, I am currently working on making the shapes crash into eachother to depict the sense of conflict and eventually I would like to allow the user to control each individual shape. with only women.
