HIMALAYA KAJAL (2017)

Kajal is an essential beauty product for Indian women. We as a community take pride in our big eyes and Kajal, a thick eyeliner, enhances our best feature. Himalaya is a local brand that manufactures Kajal, however their packaging is bleak and impersonal to the average Indian woman. I decided to take inspiration from Indian folk portraits that enlarge and emphasize the eyes and the nose and rebrand Himalaya Kajal.
